In a fantastical world lives an intelligent species that holds a deep connection to the natural world. An unseen threat, bird mites, transfer a mutated virus to a colony of swamp folk. Lichen spotted legs become gnarled, knotted roots and half of the population is rendered immobile, forced to watch themselves become one with the ground beneath them.
