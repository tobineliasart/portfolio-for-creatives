    Background

    Tobin Elias is a multidisciplinary creator residing in North Vancouver. He utilizes graphic design to develop visually engaging content in an effort to evoke conversation, social advocacy and moments of joy. He turns to illustration to convey the darker side of his curiosity, exploring natural limits and emotional responses, building community, finding ways to represent the things that can't always be seen.
