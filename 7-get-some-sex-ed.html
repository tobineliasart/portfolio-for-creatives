GET SOME! SEX ED.

EDITORIAL + GRAPHIC DESIGN

Far too often, the sex education youth receive, if any, promotes feelings of shame and is cis-hetero-male-biased.

Get Some Sex Ed. is a grassroots outreach program devoted to making sex education accessible, approachable, and inclusive for all bodies and identities. This annual report design is playful, candid and empowering to help de-stigmatize and promote healthy communication about sexual health.
